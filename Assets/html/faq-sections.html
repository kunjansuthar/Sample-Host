CRILOF products are created with skin-respecting ingredients designed to help maintain comfort, balance, and hydration. Why does CRILOF use zinc oxide instead of avobenzone?

CRILOF is a modern Avobenzone-Free sunscreen powered by Micronized Zinc Oxide for advanced broad-spectrum protection. Zinc oxide is a mineral filter that sits on the skin and reflects harmful UV rays, stays stable in sunlight, and is gentle for many sensitive skin types compared with some chemical filters.
